Team

On team page you can see team name of already existing teams and also you can create your own team. You can allocate sevral services which are created earlier, to different team and team members. Each team can consist of several team members and team members can also be repeated in several different teams. But only one unique service can be allocated to every team. Means no services are repeated here.

How to create a Team ?

step 1: On the service page click on NEW TEAM button. You will be redirected to a popup where you can add new team by entering team name.

step 2: After clicking on the NEW TEAM button, here you will find a popup where you have to enter team name. After filling the fields, click SAVE button, which will create a new team for you.

How to Migrate Team in easycloudbooks ?

step 2: Now create team by name of default project manager and add relevant services to them. kindly refer attached image for your help.

What is Default Team ?What is Default Team ?

Default team provide by Easycloudbooks, whenever any staff join easycloudbooks , they automatically add into default team. Same rule apply on new service, whenever new service create in easycloudbooks, it will automatically add into default team service section.

How to allot services to Team ?

As you can see in the picture, you can allot different services to different teams. From dropdown input field you have to select different team members and services respectively. A team can consist of several members. But remember only one service is allowed to different teams.

note: No team can have service which has already been used once earlier.

How to edit and delete Team ?

To edit the team user has been provided with Orange button as seen in picture. To delete the current team user has been provided with Red button as seen in picture.

What is error Team ?

Error team is default team provided to complete the projects with error like original service for which the project was created got deleted and therefore to complete project we may need members which are provided from error team.

Explain Access control on Team Page ?(In Role Page)

Super owner can control which user can see team page and what he can do on team page. If owner wish he can give other rights like creating, editing and deleting team to other users also.

What is Statement Team ?

This is default team which can create account statement, bill and receipt. Consider this as your accounts department team which will be able to access accounts statement page.

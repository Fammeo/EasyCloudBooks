Company

This module enables you to access all your companies information within seconds. Apart from general information like company’s name, address, industry type, legal structure, it also provides essential details such as pending projects, projects on hold, billed amount, balance pending, custom details pending if unavailable, and its manager/contact person who deals with the matter. It also informs about the relationship manager i.e., any member of your staff whom the projects of that company have been assigned.

How to import companies in Easycloudbooks?

Import many companies into Easycloudbooks at once, saving you time when you're setting up your practice. You can import Companies using a template XLS file, or a XLXS file. If you already have companies in Easycloudbooks, you can import more. Know More

How to create new Company ?

step 1: To create a new company, click on the CREATE NEW button on the company page.

step 2: You will be redirected to a form, where you need to fill up some information of the company to be created. It covers all the essential details about the particular company.

step 3: Click on SAVE CHANGES button and it will be displayed in the Company List on the first page. Once a new company is created, two new tabs are activated :- CONTACTS and LICENSE / REGISTRATION.

How to assign /change relationship manager to a particular Company?

A relationship manager could be any member of your staff whom you have specifically assigned to deal with the particular company. To assign or change a relationship manager for any company, just click on the icon shown in that column. A drop down menu will appear showing the names of all of your staff members. Select among them and press ‘OK’.

What is a legend? How is it related to a Company?

Legend is a small line, of different colours, in front of the company name.It shortens the process of managing companies, by highlighting its important aspects. Each colour of legend symbolize some special information about the particular company.
RED- This colour draws your attention towards some important issues, like projects on hold, balance pending,if any of that company.
ORANGE - It indicates that some essential information is missing, i.e., all the details of that company are not properly filled.
BLUE - Blue colour signifies success about all the required projects. It displays project completion and billing status.
GREEN - This colour shows all the pending projects of the particular company.
BLACK - Black colour ensures all the tasks, of that company, have been dealt with.

How to view details of a Company?

By clicking on the arrow in front of the company name, you can see some basic details of that company like his address, contacts he is related with, etc. However full detailed information can be obtained through mouse over. A small window is displayed showing all the essential details such as contacts- that company is associated with, number of its ongoing and pending projects, balance amount etc. Further if you want to know its contact-wise details , you will have to click on the small box, in front of the company's name.

How to view contact's short details of particular Company ?

As show in picture, you can view contact's short information by Mouse over on the contact name of the company. For example contact's address, email, phone number and projects information can be seen here. You can also view the same contact by clicking BLUE view button.

How to attach any contact to your Company ?

step 1: When you click on the arrow in front of the company name, a small ORANGE button appears ,next to its contact's name. Clicking on this orange button, will redirect you to company’s contact page.

step 2:Here you will find a blue button, named ATTACH CONTACT. On clicking this button, you will get a pop-up screen, having two different options

step 3: Existing Contact - Just by typing initials, a dropdown list will display all the existing contacts of that name. Create New Contact - If you want to attach that company to a new contact, enter details of it. Both these options will ask for the position of that contact in the particular company. Assign position and click on ATTACH CONTACT button.

How to add multiple contacts to a single Company? How?

Easycloudbooks enables you to add as many contacts to a single company, as you want. The process to add multiple contacts is same as mentioned in above question.

How to keep track of important licenses and registrations, of all Companies ?

You can easily manage the details of all the licenses and registrations of all your clients through the License/Registration feature in the Companies Module.

step 1: When you click on the EDIT Button , it will redirect you to a new Page having different options : General / Contacts / Licenses & Registration .

step 2: Now Clicking on the ADD LICENSE / REGISTRATION button, you will get on the list containing the details of all licences and registrations pertaining to your clients. It shows full detailed information of its license such as name, number, issuing authority, date to & from, applicable laws etc.

How to add a new license to the LICENSE/REGISTRATION list?

step 1: On the LICENSE/REGISTRATION page, you will find a ADD LICENSE / REGISTRATION button at the top right side.

step 2: Clicking on this button will give you a pop up form where you can fill the details of the license to be added to the list. Once you fill in all the necessary details, just click on the ADD LICENSE/REGISTRATION button.

How to get reminders for renewal dates of license/registration?

You can automatically get reminders for renewal dates once you tick on NEED REMINDER button along with the details of that license.

Can I store details of license Amendments / Renewals?

Yes, you can easily track original license as well as its amendments / renewals in the same list. Here LEGEND helps you identify both through different colours. The process to add details of amendments / renewals is same as that for original license details.

To what extent I can control users access on companies page ?(In Role Page)

You can stop access or you can give limited access or full access to users on companies page.
To Stop Access - Turn off Companies Page
To give access to only those companies on whose projects user is working - keep advance button off.

To give access to all companies - Turn on advance button Note :- You can control Create, Edit, Delete, Import from excel, custom fields related, relationship manager properties with turn on/ turn off button.

What is meaning of related companies on advance button of Companies Access ?(In Role Page)

When advance button is OFF, user will be able to view only those companies on which user is working on projects. When advance button is turned ON, user will be able to view all companies.

Can I Bookmark companies to for quick reference?

Yes you can bookmark companies and filter the companies only to show bookmarked companies. This functionality you can use to keep reference of companies you are currently working on or to keep track of important companies for faster processing or to keep track of companies requiring discussion.

Custom Field

We have introduced new feature of management of custom fields with type such as date, text, number, boolean and select. You can apply these custom fields in company, contact and projects directly and on company and projects as per services offered to company. To add custom field you need to create it first on custom field menu in available below scheduler section.
To add fields based on services you need to -
1. Create custom field in custom fields section and then
2. Link that field in service section.

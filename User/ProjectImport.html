Project Import

Easycloudbooks provides the facility to create multiple projects at a same time, through a single process. The process involves 5 simple steps to be followed :
1. Select Companies for whom projects are to be created.
2. Select Services from the list of services.(In this step, the list will display services depending on the role of the particular person and the privileges given to him)
3. Tick on Years as per the period of the service.
4. Final View displays all the details selected by you and asks for its confirmation.
5. Once confirmed, Created Projects shows all the projects created by you.
